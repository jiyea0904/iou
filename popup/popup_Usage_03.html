Request for IP-Exchange

1. How can you make request for IP-Exchange?

(You can request at the ‘request for IP-Exchange’ corner.)

  • Request for 'IP-Exchange' can be made by big IP firms, small IP firms, and individual applicants.
  • You must select the purpose of the request first, and then application date and country.
    (For Call-Incoming, your country name is automatically entered. Select the date you want to receive incomings.)
  • 1) Request Purposes
    • [A]: Call-Incoming
      • for big IP firms seeking incomings.
    • [B]: IP-Exchange
      • for big IP firms seeking general IP-Exchanges.
    • [C]: Profit-Sharing or Searching-Best
      • for small IP firms seeking Profit-Sharing or Applicants (companies, individuals) also can participate in IOU to search the competent foreign IP firms and minimize costs.
2. What is the next procedure after the request is accepted?
  • Then you will receive email notices from IOU consecutively. You will be able to find the best partner.
  • 1) 1st Notice
    • Notify that your request is received, and verify inputs.
  • 2) 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and 5th Notice
    • Notify that match-making process and start negotiations for the brokerage fees.
  • 3) 6th Notice
    • Give the final notice of completion, and inform that the invoice for payment is issued.
  • IOU automatically starts the brokerage process right after the request is accepted. The Profit-Sharing and Searching-Best will be 100% matched.
  • In the brokerage process, the IP-Exchanges have priority over the Call-Incoming.
  • Grouping will be conducted based on the country, timing, and characteristics of the IP firms.
